Who is this site for?
My site is for anyone that wants to know about my professional work experience or anyone that is curious about my work history.

Who is my target audience?
My target audience is mostly future employers.

What is my site supposed to actually do?
My site is supposed to show my work history (resume) in a professional yet creative manor.

How will I achieve the target goal of my site?
I will keep the design simple and while I create my website, I will look at the result from an employer's point of view.  After it is complete, I will have experienced professionals who look at resumes on a daily basis check it over and get their opinions for upgrades.
